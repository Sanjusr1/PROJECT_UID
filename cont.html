Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about MovieSearch.

What is MovieSearch?
MovieSearch is your go-to platform for discovering movies, tracking your watchlist, and saving your favorites. We provide details like overviews, cast, trailers, and where to watch.
How do I add movies to my Watchlist?
On any movie card or detail page, you'll see a bookmark icon. Click it to add the movie to your watchlist. Click it again to remove it.
Can I change the movie language?
Yes! On the main search page, there's a language selector where you can choose your preferred language for movie titles and overviews.
Is my data saved across sessions?
Currently, your watchlist and favorites are saved in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser data or use a different device, they might not be available. We are working on cloud synchronization!
How do I search for movies by genre?
On the main search page, use the genre filter dropdown to select a category like Action, Comedy, or Drama. The movie list will update to show matches for your selected genre.
Can I share my watchlist with friends?
Not yet, but we're working on a feature to allow sharing your watchlist via a link or social media. Stay tuned for updates!
What should I do if a movie’s details are incorrect?
If you notice incorrect details, please use the "Report an Issue" option on the movie’s detail page. Our team will review and update the information as needed.
